Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 8, 2026

Welcome to Easy Ramzan Recipe. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the App, such as:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and profile picture provided via Firebase Authentication.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored using Google Firebase. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services:

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, edit, or delete your personal data directly through the app settings. You can delete your account at any time, which will remove your associated data from our systems.

6. Children's Privacy

Easy Ramzan Recipe does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer via the official support channels mentioned in the app store.
